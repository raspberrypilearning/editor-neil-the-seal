## Customise your cars and barriers

Now make the cars and barriers your own. You'll make these changes on both the `Car`{:class="block3looks"} and `Barrier`{:class="block3looks"} sprites.

## Step 1

In the `repeat ()`{:class="block3control"} loop, change the number to decide how many cars appear. Then do the same on the `Barrier`{:class="block3looks"} sprite.

## Step 2

Decide how many points each one is worth by changing the number in the `change score by ()`{:class="block3variables"} block. Maybe some things are trickier to reach and should be worth more?

## Step 3

Choose where each type of object appears. In the `go to x: () y: ()`{:class="block3motion"} block, change the `pick random () to ()`{:class="block3operators"} numbers to set the area they spawn in — for example, keeping the cars low down so they stay on the road.

## Now run your code

Click the green flag. The town is now full of signs, cars, and barriers to smash — each worth the points you chose, in the places you picked.
