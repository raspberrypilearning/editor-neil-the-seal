## Make the word list

At the moment, the player can just hold down the space bar to smash everything at once.

You'll add a short delay — and give Neil something to say each time he smashes.

![stage](images/stage-sprite.png)

Click on the `Stage`.

Make a new list called `neil's words`{:class="block3variables"}, **For all sprites**.

Using the list on the Stage, click the `+` to add a few things for Neil to shout — as many as you like, and anything you want!

Then untick the list's checkbox to hide it from the player.
